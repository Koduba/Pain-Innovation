Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PIN platform

How do I reset my PIN password?
Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
What browsers are supported by PIN?
PIN supports the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
How do I export patient data?
Navigate to the Reports section, select your desired data range and parameters, then click "Export" to download data in CSV or PDF format.
Is PIN HIPAA compliant?
Yes, PIN is fully HIPAA compliant and uses industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect patient data.
How do I integrate PIN with our EMR system?
Contact your system administrator or our support team for assistance with EMR integration. We support standard HL7 and FHIR protocols.
What is the data retention policy?
Patient data is retained according to regulatory requirements and your organization's settings. Contact your admin for specific retention periods.
